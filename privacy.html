Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-21

This is a personal, non-commercial financial dashboard built and used by a single individual for their own private use. It is not a product, service, or business offered to the public.

What data this application accesses

When connected to a bank account via Open Banking (Enable Banking), this application reads account balance and transaction data belonging solely to its owner, for the sole purpose of displaying that data back to the owner in a personal dashboard.

Data sharing

No data is shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third party. Data is stored in a private database controlled by the owner and is not used for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond personal record-keeping.

Data retention

Data is retained for as long as the owner chooses to use the dashboard, and can be deleted at any time by the owner.

Contact

This application has no public users. There is no support channel, as it is not distributed to anyone other than its own creator.
